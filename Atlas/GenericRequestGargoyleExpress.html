Scan & Deliver Policies

Staff will process:

  • Up to 75 pages of a requested item (as long as this does not exceed the above copyright policy)
  • One chapter or article per request. Please submit separate requests for two articles/chapters.
  • You may place up to 200 requests per quarter, but the Scan & Deliver staff may only be able to process 5 requests per patron per day, depending on staffing and overall request volume.

Scan & Deliver cannot be supplied for:

  • Course Reserve materials.
  • Items held in the Special Collections Research Center.
  • Microform / microfilm.

Per Item Limitations:

(Due to copyright guidelines established in consultation with University Legal Counsel.)

  • Up to two book chapters or 20% of the entire book, whichever is less.
  • Up to two articles or 20% (whichever is less) from a single issue of a periodical or newspaper.
